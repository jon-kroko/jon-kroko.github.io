Earthquake Simulator Pro

Imagine there has been an exciting earthquake in your area and you missed it. Sh**! Now what? Are you going to live with this void in your life? Will you accept it?

No! Missing earthquakes was yesterday. Experience up-to-date earthquakes in your browser with Earthquake Simulator Pro.

Using it is very simple: Explore the map below and simulate an earthquake by double-clicking its marker. The stronger the original quake, the wilder your browser experience. Have fuaaaAAAAAAAH...
